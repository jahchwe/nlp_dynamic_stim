Welcome to the experiment!

Thank you for participating in our experiment!

We are researchers interested in how we understand other people.

Your job today is simple. There are three parts to our task.

  • 1. You will watch videos and write down anything that comes to mind about the person in the video. Truly anything about the person is a suitable answer.
  • 2. After watching the video, you will rate the person on several attributes.
  • 3. After sorting, you will create a final impression of the person in the video, deleting any words that you entered while watching the video and adding any others that you feel like to summarize who the person is.

You will repeat these three steps for each video.

The videos will be of people in a variety of situations, some of them real life situations and others make up situations with actors. In either case, we ask that you form impressions about the person as if the situation was real

In the next 3 pages, you will learn more about the 3 steps of the experiment.

Enter whatever comes to mind spontaneously. There are no limits on what you enter!
